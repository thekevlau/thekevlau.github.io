Technical Projects

  • ONE Workout (work in progress) -- A One Punch Man inspired fitness tracking web application written in React.js.

  • Server-Monitor (work in progress) -- A service for automatic server problem detection and remediation, written in Python using Sparts.

  • Project Hydro (Engineering Design Project) -- A custom, IOT-enabled sensor that seemlessly integrates into a household toilet to provide individual, real-time hydration information to a user's smartphone.

  • Embedded Software Design (Real Time Operating Systems) -- Writing a kernel for the Cortex M-3 ARM processor using C.

  • VHDL Compiler Project -- Developing a compiler for VHDL(subset) to MIPS in Java

  • Kalos -- A barbell attachment and pressure-sensing platform that tracks, analyzes and provides real-time feedback about one's weighlifting form via mobile app for injury prevention.

  • DIY Eye-Tracker -- Developed an electroencephalogram (EEG) sensor from scratch that would track a person's eye position in real time by measuring changes in potential difference across the face for less than $50.

Other Work

  • Consultant -- I have worked as a private consultant for numerous startups and corporations from across North America on business strategy, product and corporate growth, fundraising strategy, engineering practice, pitching, marketing and hiring.

  • Hack the North -- I co-founded and co-directed Canada's largest hackathon for two years.

  • WaterlUX -- I co-founded and was president of the University of Waterloo's UI/UX Design club for 2 years. I also designed the club's logo!

  • Cross-Province Rube Goldberg Machine -- As NEM Director for the provincial engineering student union (ESSCO), I led a team of 15 from 13 Universities across Ontario to make a massive rube goldberg machine to light the CN Tower Purple for two consecutive years.

  • CANDEO -- Led a team of 6 in developing a 3D braille-based smartphone-screen for the blind for Shad Valley.

Research

  • UX Design -- I worked with the Games Institute at the University of Waterloo to setup and design a Rails based web-platform for a school-wide study about touch-screen usage patterns.

  • Stem & Cancer Cell Research -- I investigated the effect of mechanical barriers on the extracellular matrix of RBMSC stem cells and HT-1080 cancer cells with respect to stem cell differentiation and cell growth and stretching respectively with the Matrix Dynamics Group at the University of Toronto.

  • Optimizing Biomechanics in Badminton -- I co-designed, led and fundraised from the private sector to run a study investigating the bio-physics of badminton and potential optimization strategies based on shuttle kinematics.
